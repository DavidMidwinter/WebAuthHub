In the far future, Mars is locked in the grip of war, caught in a centuries-long feud between the Inner and Outer Planets; a feud that mankind has no intention of joining.

However, after an energy crisis cripples the planet Earth, the humans are approached by Venus, with a tempting offer: provide us an army, and we'll save your energy grid, and your civilisation. It is impossible to refuse.

Solar Soldier follows the story of Thomas Wilkins, a human soldier sent to Mars against his will, to fight for a cause he has no attachment to against an enemy he has no hatred towards.

However, as the war escalates and his understanding of Mars, the Martians and the solar system evolve, he finds himself becoming more invested in the war, more willing to fight head on.

And even more willing to die.
